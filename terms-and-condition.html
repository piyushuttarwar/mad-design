  • Mad About Discounts is a venture of Vanderpreneurs Pvt. Ltd... The name Vanderpreneurs truly personifies the instincts of each of us founder members – we are a bunch of young entrepreneurs who, like the noble savages raid and trade from our homelands for the welfare of our own people.
    • 
												
    
												
  • The story of how M.A.D. was born - Since a long time we had been envying shoppers who could walk into a store and walk out with thousands of rupees worth of stuff at a fraction of the cost - with all the searching, bargaining and couponing one can easily make the money saved worth the time.
    • 
												
    
												
  • But, what about the naïve souls like us - those who have only few minutes to spare, and not much of rupees to stretch – So, we decided to come up with an ecommerce convenience that is even easier than hunting for online coupons or carrying a booklet and keep track of all the truck load of coupons within –
    • 
												
    
												
  • you see! lazy dogs like us need everything to be “user-friendly” and we’d love it even more if it was a complete “no non-sense, no nuisance” kind of a product - Tadaaaa! the idea thus sprung - of putting the offers on to a card and making lives simpler. But, how could we stand apart from the already available privilege cards? – after tons of PR and giving hundreds of presentations to convince the merchants we came up with offers that are valid 365 days and can be availed again and again - unlike others where an offer is valid only for limited period, can only be used once and are not valid all days a week.
    • 
												
    
												
  • Finally we designed Mad About Discounts – a card that’s full of options for everything you need with enough savings left over for stuff you don’t need but always wanted to buy!
